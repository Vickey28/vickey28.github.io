UX Case Study : Responsive redesign for a co-working space website

Project :

Website for Finding workspace near you

Role :

User Research,User Interviews, Screen Flows, Visual Design, Interaction Design

Overview :

BookmyHQ is an online marketplace that connects people who want to rent out their Workspace with people who are looking to start a new office, BookmyHQ takes a unique approach toward renting . Part of the “sharing economy,” BookmyHQ offers you someone’s Place as a workspace to rent instead of searcing . On BookmyHQ, you can find places to rent your workspace through all over the world

Introduction

There's a Workspace for Everyone

The professional world has seen a major rise in coworking spaces within the last 10 years. They buzz with camaraderie, opportunity and innovation. If you’ve ever worked at one you might relate to the unspoken feeling amongst it’s members; that somehow, we’re in this together.

More and more freelancers, small businesses, startups and non-profits are flocking to coworking spaces for the win-win benefits. Some of the top reasons include flexibility, affordability, networking opportunities and increased productivity.

Defining Problem

The current website is ineffective. It is one long scroll with distracting moving elements and unclear descriptions of the workspaces. Attracts non-members from environmental and social organizations to join.

  • Project Goals: Design a custom responsive website based on UX & UI research within a 3 week timeline.
  • Business Goals: Increase conversion and maintain occupancy for bookmyHQ by attracting non-members and retaining current members.
  • User Goals: Feel a sense of community while learning about BookmyHQ and preview the space options with the end goal of booking a tour of the office.
Discover

Competitive Analysis

Next we put together a competitive and comparative analysis. We critically evaluated the top 5 local coworking spaces from our domain research and compared certain features to determine what the competitors are lacking:

  • List of members in the office space: Non-members would like to know who they will share space with as community is an important factor within BookmyHQ.
  • Testimonials: Social proof is an important deciding factor for non-members. Conversions are often made based on reviews.
  • Events page: Community building is what makes BookmyHQ stand out amongst its competitors.
