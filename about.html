About myself

Commerce by Qualification, Designer by Choice!

Hello, I'm Hari Vignesh, a self-taught Designer passionate about designing digital experiences that leave a positive impact on people, the environment, and society. I am a native of Madurai, India, and have a background in Commerce; in which I hold a Bachelor of Commerce with computer application B.com(CA). Interest in designing, I quit my Accountant job and made a conscious decision of moving into design, an enterprise which has led me to adopt it as a full-time career and develop my skills in UX and visual design. During the past 3 years of working in diverse industries including education, health, SaaS, and non-profits projects, I have exposed myself to various challenges involving user experience and visual design.

To me, the design is a problem-solving activity. As a designer, I understand that the perfect design not only involves visual aesthetics but even more importantly, an interface that functions well. This requires a deep understanding of human behavior and psychology - the outcome of which greatly impacts the overall experience of the product, regardless of the platform.

I personally enjoy working on projects that require both strategies as well as visual design. I love working on problems from the ideation stage and seeing them through completion through pixel-perfect designs. I dream to become a domain expert in my field
